3rd Place

The winner receives 60% of the players' pot.

The second place receives 30% of the players' pot.

The third place receives 10% of the players' pot.

Your Stake ￡5

After signing-up you'll get instructions on how to transfer the ￡5 into the pot.

50% of the pot will be distributed among the Top 3 participants.

50% of the pot will be donated to a charity selected by the winner.

Every Match

Before every match put in how you expect every match ends. You can change your bet until match kick-off.

The correct score gets you 5 points, the correct winner 3 points.

Winner of each Group

Before the first group match begins put in who you'd expect to win each group. You cannot change your choice after the first match within that group began.

Each correct group winner gets you 5 points.

The Winners

Before the first match begins enter your tournment favourite. You cannot make changes once the first match began.

The correct EURO Champion gets you 30 points, 2nd place 15 points, and 3rd place 10 points.
