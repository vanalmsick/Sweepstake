3rd Place

The winner receives 60% of the players' pot.

The second place receives 30% of the players' pot.

The third place receives 10% of the players' pot.

Your Stake ￡5

After signing up you will receive instructions on how to transfer the £5 into the pot.

50% of the pot will be distributed among the Top 3 participants.

50% of the pot will be donated to a charity selected by the winner.

Every Match

Before each match, enter your predictions for that match. You can change your predictions right up until kick-off.

The correct answer is worth 5 points, the correct winner 3 points.

Group Winner

Before the first group match kicks of, predict the winner of that group. You cannot change your selection after the first match of that group has started.

Each correct group winner is worth 8 points.

The Winners

Before the first game of the tournament (i.e. Saturday June 14th – 8:00 p.m.), predict the 2024 EURO champion. Once the first game has started, you will no longer be able to make any changes.

The correct EURO Champion is worth 25 points, the runner-up 15 points.
